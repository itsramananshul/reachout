## What is ReachOut?

ReachOut automates cold outreach to recruiters. It finds jobs across 4 job boards, hunts down the hiring manager's email, generates a personalised message with your details, and sends it from your own Gmail or Outlook — all in under 5 minutes.

No shared server. No mass-blast tool. Every email comes from your real inbox, with your resume attached.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔍 Multi-Source Job Search
Search live listings from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and Handshake simultaneously. Filter by source, browse, and tick the jobs you want to target — all in one view.

### 📧 Recruiter Email Finder
For every selected job, ReachOut generates 6 email pattern variations per contact — `firstname@`, `f.lastname@`, `fi.last@`, `firstname_last@`, and more — with confidence scoring. Connect Hunter.io for even higher accuracy.

### ✍️ Personalised Email Templates
A variable-driven template editor with live preview. Every email auto-swaps recruiter name, company, job title, and your skills per send. No two emails are identical.

### 📬 Sends From Your Own Account
Connect Gmail via Google OAuth or Outlook via Microsoft OAuth. ReachOut requests only Send permission — it can never read your inbox. Emails land in recruiter inboxes from a real person, not a bulk tool.

### 📎 Resume Attachment
Upload your resume once. It's attached to every outreach email automatically.

### 📊 Campaign Dashboard
Cloud-synced dashboard tracking emails sent, companies reached, delivery rate, and full campaign history — accessible from any device.

### 🧭 4-Step Campaign Wizard
Profile builder → job selector → email composer → live send queue with per-job status tracking. The whole flow in one place.

---

## 🔒 Privacy

- Gmail and Outlook are connected via OAuth — ReachOut only requests Send permission, never read access
- Your resume is stored locally in your browser, never uploaded to any server
- API keys (Hunter.io, RapidAPI) are stored only in your account — never shared
